2022 - 6 months

On Job Training

I entered an on-the-job training program—no more simulations, this was hands-on. I learned Angular CLI, Spring Boot, even COBOL and Excel VBA. MySQL, version control, team collaboration—it was like learning how to survive in the wild after spending years in the classroom. For the final project, I worked on something real: an announcement system. We built it using Angular and integrated it with Telegram. The idea was simple but powerful: whenever a message was announced, all staff would receive it via Telegram and email. The system would then track who had read the message and who hadn’t—and report it all back to HR or the original announcer. It was a challenge. But when it worked, when the message was delivered, and the reports came back in clean and precise—I felt like I had built something that truly mattered. And this… is only the beginning.
