About Hanami

Hanami was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create beauty products that enhance your self-care rituals. Our name draws inspiration from the Japanese tradition of appreciating the transient beauty of flowers.

Each of our candles is hand-poured using sustainable soy wax and premium essential oils. Our hair care line is crafted with natural ingredients that nourish and revitalize.

We believe that taking time for yourself is an essential part of beauty, and our products are designed to help you create moments of peace in your daily routine.
